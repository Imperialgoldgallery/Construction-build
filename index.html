Our Journey

A Legacy of Excellence

From a small family business to a global construction powerhouse, our story is one of passion, innovation, and unwavering commitment to quality.

1998

The Foundation

Founded in Milan, Italy, Aurelia began as a boutique construction firm specializing in luxury residential projects. Our founder, Marco Aureliani, envisioned a company where craftsmanship and innovation would converge.

2005

European Expansion

Aurelia expanded across Europe, completing landmark projects in Paris, London, and Barcelona. Our signature blend of Italian artistry and modern engineering earned international acclaim.

2012

Global Presence

Opening offices in Dubai, Singapore, and New York, Aurelia became a truly global construction leader. Our sustainable building practices set new industry standards.

2026

The Future of Luxury

Today, Aurelia continues to push boundaries with smart building technology, carbon-neutral construction, and designs that harmonize with nature. The legacy continues.
