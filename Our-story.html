Our Story

For over two decades, Aurelia Building Construction has been shaping skylines and building legacies — one project at a time.

Our Beginning

Built on Passion,
Driven by Purpose

Aurelia Building Construction was founded in 1998 by visionary builder Marcus Aurelius Chen, who believed that every structure tells a story. Starting from a modest workshop with just five dedicated craftsmen, Aurelia was born from the conviction that construction is not merely about erecting buildings — it's about creating spaces where life unfolds.

Our founder's philosophy was simple yet revolutionary: treat every project as if it were our own home. This ethos became the cornerstone of everything we do, guiding us through two decades of growth, innovation, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, Aurelia stands as one of the region's most trusted construction firms, having delivered over 500 projects ranging from luxury residences to commercial landmarks, each bearing our signature mark of quality and attention to detail.

Our Journey Through the Years

The Foundation

Aurelia secures its first large-scale commercial contract, delivering the 42-story Meridian Tower two months ahead of schedule.
